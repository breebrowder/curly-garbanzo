MEET YOUR COACH

Get to know Tawny from someone else’s POV A bio written about Tawny, not by her, so you know it’s real
 

Tawny Ann De La Peña is a self-proclaimed and actual bad bitch who is unafraid to take her West Coast energy wherever the wind blows her. She made a name for herself as a copywriter but decided to take on a different challenge — life coaching.

   

Why? She says there are so many reasons she shouldn't have "made it" — from drug addiction and cancer to domestic and emotional abuse; she didn’t get into any college she applied to in high school; the list goes on. But she succeeded anyway.

   

Not only did she get into a university by way of community college, but she’s been sober for more than a decade and in remission for more than five years. Tawny’s work has garnered accolades like Peabody nominations, Emmys, and Addy awards. Her work is also featured in publications like MSNBC, ABC, Business Insider, and more.

   

Tawny genuinely works to become a better version of herself every single day (and it shows). Her greatest hope is to help others achieve the same kind of transformation she did. She is living proof that you can feel better and do better — and ultimately change the way you experience life.

   
“The life I live now is better than the one I dreamed up as a kid. You don’t ever gotta settle for less than you deserve. And I’m down to help you claim your crown like the bad bitch you are.”
 
- Tawny Ann De La Peña
